## Conteudo (HTML original)

<p>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; font-size: 12pt;">Na parreira os ramos

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; font-size: 12.0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Ramos produtivos

15:3 RA)</span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">,

mais fruto ainda.”</span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; font-size: 7.5pt;"> Jo 15:2 RA)</span></p>
