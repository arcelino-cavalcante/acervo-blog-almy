## Conteudo (HTML original)

<p><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; font-size: 12pt;">A unidade do corpo de Cristo é transcendente e imanente, isto é, tanto

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 107%;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

pois geram alegria no “coração” de Deus.<o:p></o:p></span></p>
