<p></p><ul style="text-align: left;"><li>&nbsp;<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">“Tu és o mais formoso dos filhos dos homens;

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; </span>Os filhos de Corá nos apresentam

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="text-align: left; text-indent: -18pt;"></p><ol style="text-align: left;"><li><span style="text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, serif;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-feature-settings: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-optical-sizing: auto; font-size: 7pt; font-stretch: normal; font-variant-alternates: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-position: normal; font-variation-settings: normal; line-height: normal;">&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; text-indent: -18pt;">Boca

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; </span>Adotando estas posturas, não
precisaremos correr atrás das bênçãos, pois elas virão ao&nbsp; nosso encontro.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; </span>Tratando bem quem não merece ser bem
tratado, atraímos para nós a bondade de Deus, pois Jesus nos ensina: <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"></p><ul style="text-align: left;"><li><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; text-indent: -18pt;">“Bem-aventurados os misericordiosos, porque

6:35 RA)</li></ul><p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; mso-font-kerning: 0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">Amando

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"></p><ul style="text-align: left;"><li><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; mso-font-kerning: 0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif; text-indent: -18pt;">“Bem-aventurados os que têm fome e sede de

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; mso-font-kerning: 0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">Busquemos

padrões de satisfação espiritual.</span></p>
