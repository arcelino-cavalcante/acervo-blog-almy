<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; mso-font-kerning: 0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</o:p></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">Ter um

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; O

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; mso-font-kerning: 0pt;"><o:p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; </o:p></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;, serif;">Procure promover situações de celebração a

<p class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span style="color: black; font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif; mso-font-kerning: 0pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Os filhos de Corá estimulavam&nbsp; os povos baterem palmas pra Deus, celebrá-lo com vozes de júbilo, isto é, grande
